Meet the Team

Yuli Suliman, Saar Orus, and Adi Rozenberg are the founders of JOBIT.

We are third-year computer science students at Tel Aviv-Yafo Academic College. As students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the challenges of finding work that could fit into our busy schedules. We created JOBIT to provide a solution for students and anyone else seeking flexible, non-permanent work. Our passion for helping others find work that fits their lifestyle and our commitment to providing a top-quality platform have made JOBIT a success.
